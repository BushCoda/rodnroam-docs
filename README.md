## 🐟 About Rod N Roam

Rod N Roam is built for Australian fishing enthusiasts. The app helps you:

- **Discover** top fishing spots around Australia
- **Log** your catches with photos, species, and location data
- **Plan** trips with tide charts, weather forecasts, and local regulations
- **Connect** with a community of fellow anglers
