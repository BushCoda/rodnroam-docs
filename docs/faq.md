# Frequently Asked Questions ❓

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common things our users ask about Rod N Roam.

---

## General

### Is Rod N Roam free to download?
Yes! Downloading Rod N Roam is completely free on both the App Store and Google Play. Some premium features (see below) are available via an optional subscription.

### Do I need an account to use the app?
You can browse the **Explore** map without logging in, but you'll need a free account to log catches, save spots, plan trips, and access the community features.

### What is Rod N Roam Premium?
Rod N Roam Premium is our optional subscription that unlocks extra features:
- Unlimited offline map downloads
- Advanced weather and tide analytics
- Ad-free experience
- Priority customer support

Premium costs **$4.99/month** or **$39.99/year** (billed in AUD). You can try it free for 14 days.

## Account & Profile

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
1. On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password**.
2. Enter the email address linked to your account.
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link (check your spam folder too, mate!).
4. Follow the link and set a new password.

### Can I change my username?
Yes. Go to **Profile → Edit Profile** and update your display name. Note: your username (used in community features) can only be changed once every 30 days.

### How do I delete my account?
We're sorry to see you go! To delete your account:
1. Go to **Settings → Account**.
2. Tap **Delete Account**.
3. Confirm by entering your password.

All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

## Fishing Spots & Maps

### Why can't I see the map properly?
Make sure you have an active internet connection. For offline use, you'll need to download map tiles in advance via **Settings → Offline Maps**.

### Can I add a new fishing spot?
Absolutely! Tap the **+** icon on the Explore map, then tap **Add Spot**. Fill in the details and submit – our team reviews new spots before they go live (usually within 48 hours).

### Are fishing regulations up to date?
We do our best to keep regulation data current for all states and territories, but **always check with your local fisheries authority** before heading out. Regulations can change, and it's your responsibility to fish legally.

## Catch Log

### Can I log a catch without internet?
Yes! Catches are saved locally on your device when you're offline and automatically synced to your account when you reconnect.

### Can I edit or delete a logged catch?
Yes. Open the catch from your **Catch Log**, tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top right corner, and select **Edit** or **Delete**.

### Is my catch log private?
By default, your catches are visible to the Rod N Roam community. You can change this to **Friends Only** or **Private** in **Profile → Privacy Settings**.

## Tides & Weather

### Where does the weather and tide data come from?
Weather data is sourced from the **Bureau of Meteorology (BOM)** and updated every 30 minutes. Tide predictions are calculated using official Australian hydrographic data.

### Why is the tide time different from what I expected?
Make sure your spot is set to the correct location and that your phone's timezone is set accurately. Tide charts show times in **local time** for the selected spot.

## Technical

### The app is running slowly. What can I do?
Try the following:
1. Close and reopen the app.
2. Check your internet connection.
3. Clear the app cache via **Settings → Storage → Clear Cache**.
4. Ensure the app is updated to the latest version.

### What devices are supported?
- **iOS:** iPhone and iPad running iOS 14 or later.
- **Android:** Devices running Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later.
