# Getting Started with Rod N Roam 🎣

Welcome aboard, legend! This guide will get you up and running with Rod N Roam in no time.

---

## 📱 Step 1 – Download the App

Rod N Roam is available on both iOS and Android – no charge to download.

| Platform | Link |
|---|---|
| **iOS** (iPhone & iPad) | [Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com) |
| **Android** | [Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com) |

> **Minimum requirements:**
> - iOS 14 or later
> - Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later
> - An active internet connection for first-time setup

---

## 👤 Step 2 – Create Your Account

1. Open the app after installation.
2. Tap **Sign Up** on the welcome screen.
3. Enter your name, email address, and a secure password.
4. Tap **Create Account** and check your email for a verification link.
5. Click the verification link, then come back to the app and log in.

> **Already have an account?** Just tap **Log In** and enter your credentials.

---

## 🗺️ Step 3 – Set Up Your Profile

Once you're logged in, it's time to personalise your experience:

1. Tap the **Profile** icon in the bottom navigation bar.
2. Add a profile photo (optional but great for the community!).
3. Enter your home state or territory – this helps the app surface relevant fishing spots, local regulations, and weather.
4. Select your preferred fishing styles (e.g. freshwater, saltwater, fly fishing).
5. Tap **Save**.

---

## 📍 Step 4 – Allow Location Access

Rod N Roam uses your location to show nearby fishing spots and provide accurate tidal and weather data.

- When prompted, tap **Allow While Using App**.
- You can adjust location permissions at any time in your device **Settings → Rod N Roam**.

---

## 🎯 Step 5 – Find Your First Spot

1. Tap the **Explore** tab (map pin icon).
2. Use the map or search bar to find a location near you.
3. Tap a spot marker to see details: species, access notes, regulations, and recent catches from the community.
4. Tap **Save Spot** to add it to your favourites.

---

## ✅ You're Ready to Go!

From here, check out the [Features & Walkthrough](features.md) guide to discover everything Rod N Roam can do for your next trip.

---

*Tight lines and happy fishing! 🐟*
