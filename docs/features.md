# App Features & Walkthrough 🗺️

Rod N Roam is packed with tools to make every fishing trip a success. Here's a rundown of all the key features.

---

## 🗺️ Explore – Find Fishing Spots

The **Explore** tab is your interactive map of fishing locations across Australia.

- **Search** by suburb, river, lake, or postcode.
- **Filter** spots by fishing type: freshwater, saltwater, estuary, or offshore.
- **View spot details** including GPS coordinates, access notes, species found, and community photos.
- **Save favourites** to quickly revisit your best spots.
- Spots are colour-coded by **catch rating** (community-sourced).

---

## 📓 Catch Log – Track Your Catches

Keep a detailed diary of every fish you land.

1. Tap the **+** button on the home screen or the Catch Log tab.
2. Select the **species** from a searchable list of Australian fish.
3. Enter the **weight and length** (optional but satisfying!).
4. Add a **photo** from your camera or gallery.
5. The app auto-fills the **location** using your GPS.
6. Add **notes** – water conditions, bait used, time of day.
7. Tap **Save Catch**.

Your catch history is displayed on a personal timeline and plotted on the map.

---

## 🌊 Tides & Weather

Never get caught out by a surprise southerly again.

- **7-day weather forecast** for your saved spots and current location.
- **Tide charts** with high/low tide times, heights, and a graphical curve view.
- **Wind speed and direction** overlays on the map.
- **Moon phase** indicator – great for planning around fish feeding patterns.
- Forecasts update every 30 minutes using Bureau of Meteorology data.

---

## 📋 Trip Planner

Plan your next adventure with the built-in Trip Planner.

1. Go to the **Trips** tab and tap **New Trip**.
2. Enter a trip name and select your target date(s).
3. Add fishing spots from your favourites or search the map.
4. The app checks **weather forecasts** and **tide conditions** for your chosen dates and spots.
5. Add a **packing checklist** with items like bait, rods, sunscreen, and snacks.
6. Invite fishing mates via the **Share Trip** button (they'll need a Rod N Roam account).

---

## 🐠 Species Guide

Not sure what you just pulled up? The Species Guide has you covered.

- Browse or search **Australian fish species** with high-quality photos and descriptions.
- View **identification tips**, habitat information, and typical size ranges.
- Check **bag limits and size restrictions** for your state or territory (updated regularly).
- Learn the best **techniques and baits** for each species.

---

## 👥 Community & Social

Connect with other Aussie anglers.

- **Activity Feed** – see recent catches from anglers in your area.
- **Comment and react** on catches to share tips and encouragement.
- **Leaderboards** – friendly competition by species, weight, or number of catches.
- **Groups** – join or create groups for fishing clubs, mates, or regional communities.

---

## 🔔 Notifications

Stay in the loop with smart alerts.

- **Fishing condition alerts** – get notified when conditions at your saved spots look great.
- **Community activity** – new catches near your favourite spots.
- **Trip reminders** – we'll remind you the day before a planned trip.

You can customise notification preferences in **Settings → Notifications**.

---

## ⚙️ Settings

Fine-tune Rod N Roam to suit your style.

- Switch between **metric and imperial** units.
- Set your **home region** for tailored content.
- Manage **privacy** – choose what's visible on your public profile.
- **Offline mode** – download spot data for areas without mobile coverage.
- **Account management** – update your email, password, or delete your account.
