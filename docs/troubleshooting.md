# Troubleshooting & Support 🛠️

Having a bit of trouble? No worries – this guide covers the most common issues and how to fix them.

---

## App Won't Open or Keeps Crashing

**Try these steps in order:**

1. **Force close** the app and reopen it.
2. **Restart your device** – works more often than you'd think!
3. **Check for updates** – an outdated app is a common cause of crashes. Visit the App Store or Google Play and update Rod N Roam.
4. **Reinstall the app** – uninstall Rod N Roam, then download it fresh. Don't worry, your account data is saved to our servers.

> If crashes continue after reinstalling, please [contact support](#contact-support) and let us know your device model and OS version.

---

## Can't Log In

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| Forgot password | Tap **Forgot Password** on the login screen and follow the reset email |
| Email not recognised | Double-check the email – you may have signed up with a different address |
| Account locked | After 10 failed login attempts, accounts are temporarily locked for 15 minutes |
| Verification email not received | Check your spam/junk folder, or tap **Resend Verification Email** on the login screen |

---

## Map Not Loading or Showing Blank Screen

1. **Check your internet connection** – the map requires data on first load.
2. **Force refresh** – pull down on the map to trigger a reload.
3. **Download offline maps** – if you're heading somewhere remote, pre-download maps in **Settings → Offline Maps** while you still have signal.
4. **Clear the map cache** – go to **Settings → Storage → Clear Map Cache**.

---

## Location Not Working or Inaccurate

1. Make sure **Location Services** is enabled for Rod N Roam:
   - **iOS:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Rod N Roam → **While Using the App**
   - **Android:** Settings → Apps → Rod N Roam → Permissions → Location → **Allow only while using the app**
2. Move to an open area away from tall buildings or dense tree cover.
3. Ensure **Wi-Fi and mobile data** are both turned on to assist GPS accuracy.

---

## Weather or Tides Not Updating

- Data refreshes every 30 minutes. Pull down on the screen to force a manual refresh.
- Check your internet connection.
- If a specific location shows stale data, it may be outside our supported coverage area. Try a nearby location.
- **Timezone issues:** Ensure your phone's timezone matches the region you're viewing.

---

## Catch Log Not Syncing

- Catches saved offline will sync automatically once you reconnect to the internet.
- If a catch isn't appearing after reconnecting, go to **Settings → Account → Sync Now**.
- If the problem persists, log out and back in – this triggers a full sync.

---

## Premium Subscription Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| Can't access Premium features after subscribing | Log out and log back in to refresh your subscription status |
| Charged but subscription not active | Check your purchase in the App Store / Google Play and tap **Restore Purchases** in **Settings → Premium** |
| Cancel subscription | Cancel via your App Store or Google Play subscription settings (not within the app) |

> **Refunds:** Subscription refunds are handled by Apple or Google, not Rod N Roam directly. Contact them through your purchase history.

---

## Reporting a Bug

Found something that doesn't look right? We'd love to know!

1. Go to **Settings → Help → Report a Bug**.
2. Describe what happened and what you were doing when it occurred.
3. Attach a screenshot if you can.
4. Tap **Submit**.

Our dev team reviews all bug reports and we'll get back to you if we need more info.

---

## Contact Support

Can't find a fix here? Our support team is here to help.

- 📧 **Email:** support@rodnroam.com.au
- 💬 **In-App Chat:** Go to **Settings → Help → Live Chat** (available Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm AEST)
- 🌐 **Help Centre:** [help.rodnroam.com.au](https://help.rodnroam.com.au)

When contacting support, please include:
- Your **device model** and **operating system version**
- The **app version** (found in Settings → About)
- A **description of the issue** and steps to reproduce it
- Any **screenshots or screen recordings** that might help

We aim to respond to all emails within **1 business day**.
