# Community Guidelines — Rod&Roam

**Effective date:** 2026-03-21  
**Contact:** support@rodnroam.com.au

**Version history:** v1.0 – 2026-03-21 (initial release)

---

Rod&Roam is built for a safe, helpful angling community. These guidelines apply to public posts, comments, profiles, and direct messages.

## 1. Be respectful

No harassment, bullying, hate speech, threats, or discrimination. Keep disagreements civil.

## 2. No illegal or unsafe content

Do not encourage unlawful fishing activity, trespass, or unsafe behaviour. Do not post content that could put others at risk (for example, instructions to ignore weather warnings).

## 3. Protect privacy and location safety

Do not share personal information about others without permission. Be mindful when sharing exact locations, especially private property, culturally sensitive areas, or dangerous access points.

## 4. No scams, spam, or impersonation

Do not use Rod&Roam to run scams, post deceptive links, manipulate users, or send spam messages. Do not impersonate others or misrepresent affiliations.

## 5. Post only content you have the right to share

Only post photos, text, and location information you have permission to share. Respect copyright and trade marks.

## 5A. Messaging expectations

These guidelines also apply to direct messages. Don't send harassment, threats, explicit content, or spam. If someone messages you inappropriately, use blocking/reporting tools and contact support@rodnroam.com.au for urgent issues.

## 6. Reporting and enforcement

You can report content or users in-app. We may remove content, limit visibility, suspend accounts, or take other action where necessary to enforce these guidelines and our Terms. For appeals or urgent issues, contact support@rodnroam.com.au.
