# Community Guidelines

Effective Date: 2026-03-21

Welcome to our community! We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members. Here are some guidelines to help maintain a positive atmosphere:

## Be Respectful
Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, discrimination, and hate speech will not be tolerated.

## Stay on Topic
Keep discussions relevant to the community. Off-topic posts may be deleted.

## No Spam
Avoid posting spam or self-promotion. This includes excessive links to personal websites or services.

## Report Issues
If you see something that concerns you, please report it to the moderators. Your help in maintaining community standards is appreciated.

## Have Fun
Enjoy your time here and contribute positively to the community!
