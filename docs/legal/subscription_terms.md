## 1. Billing and renewal

Paid subscriptions are billed through the app store where you purchased the subscription (Apple App Store or Google Play). Subscriptions generally auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period (store rules apply).

## 2. Managing and cancelling subscriptions

You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple/Google account settings. Deleting the App does not automatically cancel your subscription.

## 3. Free trials

If you start a free trial, you will be charged when the trial ends unless you cancel before the trial end date/time via your app store settings.

## 4. Price changes and feature changes

We may change subscription offerings, pricing, or included features from time to time. Any changes will be handled through the app store's processes and disclosures and will apply at renewal unless otherwise stated by the store.

## 5. Refunds and cancellations

Refund requests are generally handled by Apple or Google under their policies. Nothing limits your rights under the Australian Consumer Law.

### 5.1 Subscriptions purchased via Apple/Google

Where purchases are made through the Apple App Store or Google Play, refunds are generally handled by the relevant app store under its policies. Please request refunds through your app store account.

### 5.2 Cancellations

You can cancel subscriptions through your app store settings. Cancellation stops future renewals; access may continue until the end of your current billing period (store rules apply).

### 5.3 If you think you were charged incorrectly

If you believe there has been an error (for example, duplicate charge), contact support@rodnroam.com.au and we will assist where we can, including by providing information needed for your app store request.

### 5.4 Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this policy excludes, restricts, or modifies any rights you may have under the Australian Consumer Law that cannot lawfully be excluded.
