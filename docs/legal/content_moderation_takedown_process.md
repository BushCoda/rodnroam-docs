# Content Moderation & Takedown Process

**Effective Date: 2026-03-21**

## Introduction
This document outlines the process for content moderation and take-down requests.

## Process Overview
1. **Review Request**: All requests are reviewed by the moderation team.
2. **Decision Making**: The team decides on the appropriate action based on our content guidelines.
3. **Notification**: Affected users are notified of the decision, if applicable.
4. **Action**: The content is moderated or taken down according to the decision.

## Appeals
Users may appeal any moderation decision by contacting support.

## Conclusion
We are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful community, and we take these processes seriously.
