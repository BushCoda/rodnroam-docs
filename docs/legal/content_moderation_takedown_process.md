## 1. Reporting

Users can report content, messages, or accounts through in-app reporting tools. If you cannot access in-app reporting, contact support@rodnroam.com.au.

## 2. Review

Reports may be reviewed using automated tools and/or human moderators. We consider context, severity, and potential harm.

## 3. Actions we may take

- Remove content or restrict visibility.
- Warn users or apply feature limitations.
- Suspend or terminate accounts for repeated or serious breaches.
- Preserve information and cooperate with law enforcement where required or authorised by law.

## 4. Appeals

Where practicable, you may appeal a moderation decision by contacting support@rodnroam.com.au with your username and details of the decision.

## 4A. Online safety (public posts and messages)

Rod&Roam includes public posting and direct messaging. We aim to reduce unlawful and harmful content and provide reporting tools. We do not allow:

- Child sexual abuse material or any sexual content involving minors.
- Credible threats of violence or content that incites violence.
- Serious harassment, stalking, or targeted abuse.
- Content encouraging unlawful fishing, trespass, or dangerous activity.

Where we reasonably believe there is an immediate risk of harm or suspected illegal activity, we may take urgent action (including account restriction) and may preserve information and cooperate with law enforcement where required or authorised by law.

## 5. Illegal content and child safety

We do not allow child sexual abuse material or other illegal content. If we become aware of content that may be illegal, we may remove it and report it to relevant authorities where required.
