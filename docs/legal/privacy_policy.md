This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose personal information in connection with Rod&Roam. We aim to comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

## 1. Personal information we collect

- Account details (email, username/handle, profile photo, settings).
- User content (catch logs, photos, posts, comments, and messages).
- Location information you enter/share and (if enabled) your device location (precise or approximate).
- Device and usage data (app version, diagnostics/crash logs, analytics events).
- Subscription status and purchase identifiers from Apple/Google (we do not receive your full card details).

## 2. Public information and direct messages

If you post publicly, your posts and certain profile information may be visible to other users and may be shared by them. If you send direct messages, message content will be visible to the sender and recipient(s) and may be processed by us and our service providers to deliver messages and to prevent abuse and enforce our policies.

## 3. How we collect personal information

We collect information directly from you (when you sign up, use features, post content, message other users, or contact support) and automatically through the App (such as device and analytics data). We may also receive information from third-party sign-in providers (Apple/Google) and app stores.

## 4. How we use personal information

- Provide and operate the App (including community features and direct messaging).
- Personalise your experience and deliver recommendations.
- Maintain security, prevent abuse, troubleshoot, and improve the App.
- Communicate with you about the service and support.
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms and policies.

## 5. AI and research use (opt-in)

If you opt in, we may use relevant catch logs, photos, location information you choose to share, and associated metadata to develop, train, validate, and improve our AI/ML models and analytics. You can change your opt-in settings at any time (applies going forward). We may also use aggregated or de-identified information for product improvement and reporting.

## 6. When we disclose personal information

- Service providers (cloud hosting, analytics, customer support, mapping/weather, messaging delivery).
- Apple/Google (subscription management and fraud prevention, as applicable).
- Researchers/fisheries partners only where you opt in and the arrangement is described to you.
- Regulators/law enforcement where required or authorised by law.

**Overseas disclosures:** Some service providers may store or access information outside Australia. Where practicable, we will identify likely countries (for example: [list countries]).

## 6A. Messages: processing and retention

Direct messages are processed to deliver messages and to help detect and address spam, abuse, and policy violations. We may retain message data for a limited period and/or in backups for security, dispute handling, and legal compliance, even if you delete messages, subject to our retention practices and legal obligations.

## 7. Direct marketing

We will only send marketing messages where permitted by law (including obtaining consent where required). You can opt out at any time using the unsubscribe function or by contacting support@rodnroam.com.au.

## 8. Data security and retention

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. We keep information only for as long as needed for the purposes in this policy, unless we are required or authorised by law to retain it longer.

## 9. Access, correction, and complaints

You can request access to, or correction of, personal information we hold about you by contacting support@rodnroam.com.au. If you have a privacy complaint, contact us and we will respond within a reasonable time. If you are not satisfied, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).
