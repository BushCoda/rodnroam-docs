## Privacy Policy

### What Information We Collect

**Account information:**
- Name, email address, and password (encrypted)
- Profile photo (if provided)
- Home state/territory and fishing preferences

**Usage data:**
- Fishing spots you view, save, or create
- Catch log entries (species, size, location, photos, notes)
- Trip plans and checklists
- In-app interactions (comments, reactions, group activity)

**Location data:**
- GPS coordinates when you use the Explore map or log a catch
- We only collect location data while the App is in use (not in the background), unless you explicitly enable background location for condition alerts

**Device data:**
